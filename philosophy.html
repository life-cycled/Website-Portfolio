What is most important to me is growing as a person and as a soul, not only gaining knowledge but acquiring an authentic sense of self. It is astonishing to think think that nothing has changed, and then to look back on oneself from a few years ago and realize the differences. I believe that this growth is dependent on putting yourself through new experiences. The challenges I have encountered in the past have played a defining role in shaping my character, and I look forward to the impact they will continue to have on my future self.
